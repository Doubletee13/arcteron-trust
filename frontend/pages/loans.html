Personal Lending

Borrow Smarter. Live Better.

Whether you're buying a home, purchasing a vehicle, or financing an education, Arcteron provides competitive rates and fast, local decisions.

Home Mortgages

Finance Your Piece of New England

Dedicated local underwriters who understand the Boston housing market from Beacon Hill to the suburbs.

Fixed-Rate Mortgages

Lock in your rate and your payment for 15, 20, or 30 years. Perfect for long-term stability in your forever home.

Adjustable-Rate (ARM)

Lower initial rates for the first 5, 7, or 10 years. An excellent strategy if you plan to move or refinance within a decade.

HELOCs

Access the equity in your home with a Home Equity Line of Credit. Pay only for what you use, ideal for renovations or major expenses.

Auto Loans

Up to 100% financing on new and used vehicles. Fast pre-approvals via our mobile app give you buying confidence before you visit the dealership.

Personal Loans

Unsecured loans up to $50,000 for debt consolidation, major purchases, or unexpected expenses with predictable fixed monthly payments.

Student Loans

Bridge the gap for higher education with flexible private student loans. Co-signer options available and rate discounts for automated payments.
