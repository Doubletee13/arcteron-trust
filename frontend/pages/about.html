Our Story

Banking Built on Integrity

Since 2004, Arcteron Trust has served the New England community by combining the personalized service of a private bank with modern digital innovation.

Our History

Rooted in Boston

Arcteron Trust was founded twenty years ago in Boston's Financial District with a simple conviction: banking should be personal, transparent, and built to endure.

We successfully navigated the 2008 financial crisis without requiring federal bailout funds, proving the strength of our conservative risk management. Today, with over $2.8 billion in assets under management, we continue to serve thousands of families and businesses across New England.
